–Featured Bird–

Yellow-rumped Warbler

  1. The most abundant and widespread wood warbler in North America.
  2. Although their colours are subdued in winter these birds absolutely *pop* in spring.
  3. Audubon's (yellow throat) and Myrtle (white throat) are two races of yellow-rumped warbler -- both occur in Alberta.
