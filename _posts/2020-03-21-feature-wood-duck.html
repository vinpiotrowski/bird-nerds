–Featured Bird–

Wood Duck

  1. Males are iridescent chestnut and green, with ornate patterns on nearly every feather.
  2. These birds live in wooded swamps, where they nest in holes in trees.
  3. The wood duck is the only North American duck that regularly produces two broods in one year.
