Bird nerds notes

We headed out after work for some birding and ended up having one of our best expeditions to date! There was an abundance of activity in the Weaselhead as soon as we arrived. Northern Flickers were laughing and there were downys everywhere. We also saw beavers, squirrels, chipmunks, hares, and deer (so basically all the mammals). We saw two new species: dark-eyed junco and American tree sparrow.
