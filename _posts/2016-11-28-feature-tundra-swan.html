–Featured Bird–

Tundra Swan

  1. It is larger than a snow goose; smaller than a trumpeter swan.
  2. The high-pitched, quivering oo-oo-whoo is constantly repeated by migrating flocks.
  3. It is one of the top 10 largest birds in Alberta.
