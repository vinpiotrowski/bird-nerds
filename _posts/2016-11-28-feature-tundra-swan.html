–Featured Bird–

Tundra Swan

  1. Larger than a Snow Goose; smaller than a Trumpeter Swan.
  2. High-pitched, quivering oo-oo-whoo is constantly repeated by migrating flocks.
  3. It's in the top 10 largest Alberta birds list.
