–Featured Bird–

Sharp-tailed Grouse

  1. Sharp-tailed grouse are related to 'prairie chickens' but often get mislabeled.
  2. Males display to attract females on communal dancing grounds called leks.
  3. Sometimes referred to as fire grouse or fire bird by Native Americans.
