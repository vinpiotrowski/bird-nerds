–Featured Bird–

Sharp-tailed Grouse

  1. Males display to attract females on communal dancing grounds called leks.
  2. Some Native American tribes have incorporated elements of the birds’ displays into their traditional dances.
  3. Sharp-tailed grouse sometimes build tunnels through snow for their night roosts.
