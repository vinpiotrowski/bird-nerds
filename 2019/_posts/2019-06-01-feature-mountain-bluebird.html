–Featured Bird–

Mountain Bluebird

  1. Mountain bluebirds are fairly small thrushes with a bright cerulean colour.
  2. Easy to observe roadside pairs throughout much of Alberta from March - September.
  3. Nest boxes have boosted populations and provided insight into the bird's behavior.
